The Name

sùil — Scottish Gaelic for "eye" and "hope." Historically rooted in the idea of the sun as the eye of the sky. The practice: diagnose what's actually happening, then build the path forward.

Working Style

The career history reflects this: NGO leadership at 300-person scale, space industry business development, data analysis, language teaching, construction industry compliance. The breadth is the asset — organising everything under one identity, the sùil, provides two clear entry points: organisations and individuals.
